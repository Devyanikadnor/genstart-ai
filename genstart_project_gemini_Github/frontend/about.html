About GenStart

GenStart is an AI-powered multi-agent platform that acts as your complete startup team – CEO, Product Manager, Developer, and Marketing Strategist – all in one place. You type an idea, GenStart generates a full startup plan.

What GenStart Does

  • Refines your idea into a clear problem and solution.
  • Designs product features, user personas, and MVP scope.
  • Suggests tech stack, architecture, and sample APIs.
  • Creates brand name, tagline, and launch strategy.
  • Summarizes everything into a 3-slide pitch.

Built For

  • Gen AI-thon (Hackathon by ML Nashik).
  • Students who want to turn ideas into startups quickly.
  • Founders who need a “virtual co-founder” to plan their product.
